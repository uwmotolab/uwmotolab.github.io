Our Mission


Equipped with a shared passion for design, innovation, and two-wheel rides, the Motolab team has gathered to model, build, and race electric motorbikes and break barriers into a fast, furious, and green future.


Design.

Draft, execute, iterate. We want to know our machine we before we grow our machine.


Build.

Roll out the blueprint. Machine shopping, parts assembly, and a whole lot of heart.


Race.

Compete in one of the US' premier zero-emissions motorcycle road racing competition.

We design and build with purpose, and with an end-goal in mind.

Our goal is to create motorbikes to compete annually in the eMotoRacing competition, where the fastest zero-emissions motorbike teams gather to see their creations fire up the track. Learn more here:


eMotoRacing allows us to come together once a year to tune performance settings, muscle last minute part swaps, challenge our agile problem solving skills, and release our dialled machine on race day.

We'll see you there.
